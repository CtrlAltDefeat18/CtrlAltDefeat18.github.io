Who We Are

Vision: To create a future where digital equity empowers underserved youth and communities in rural and township areas.

Mission: To bridge the digital divide through AI-driven education, digital skills training, and community-driven initiatives.

What We Do

  • Cyber Awareness & Online Safety
  • Offline-first Learning Platform via Raspberry Pi (chatbot, e-library, training modules)
  • Freemium training (TBC)
  • Community Partnerships (e.g., RUCE)

Our Impact

  • Raise cyber awareness for mature adults — target: 50 by Sept 2025
  • Pilot offline-first platform with 10–20 learners from Makhanda schools
  • Tutor & mentor Grade 8–11 learners in business skills, entrepreneurship, career guidance
